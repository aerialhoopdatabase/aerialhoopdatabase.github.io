Aerial Hoop Database

Welcome to my free Aerial Hoop Database. ü§ç

This website is a spare time project and is intended to help others during their aerial hoop journey. It was created independently of the Aerial Dance Studio and only serves as a support to remember learned figures. The dictionary is not intended as a replacement for the courses! For safety reasons, the figures should always be performed in the presence of an instructor when trying for the first time.

Note: Most figures in level 6 don't have universally used names, which is why some of them have been named by myself with inspiration from poledance and other aerial disciplines.
